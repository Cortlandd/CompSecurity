360 Security User Experience Improvement Program

Welcome to the 360 Security User Experience Improvement Program! In order to enhance your user experience for this product, 360 will conduct statistical analysis of how users interact with the product and each of its functions . Through such analysis, we are able to continuously enhance your user experience and the performance of the product, as well as to improve functionality and introduce innovative security features that benefit users. Our statistical analysis, we only focus on content specific to 360 products, and will never collect users' personal data.

Usage Statistics of Product Functions

In order to attentively respond to the development of new malware and Trojan viruses on different mobile platforms, as well as to maximize satisfaction of primary customer needs, we strive to continue to improve our product functionality. This is why we collect statistics on the use of each function, which represents the number of users for a particular function. This helps us to more accurately determine the importance of each function, and guides us in adjusting its priority for research and development. During this process, we only analyze the usage frequency statistics of product functions, and this will never involve users' personal data.

Product Upgrade Statistics

A server connection is required for 360 Security updates. 360 Security checks for program updates, then updates programs and the necessary Trojan horse virus library files, in order to increase defenses against Trojan viruses and to enhance your user experience.

In order to distinguish between different mobile platforms and to provide the most appropriate and useful upgrade services, we ask you to provide your software version number, cell phone IMEI number, phone model information, and system version number. The server uses this data to determine whether an upgrade is necessary, and reports this information to 360 Security so the upgrade may be completed. Only 1% of collected data will be upload to the server. For example, when a user enables automatic updates or manually connects to the network for updates, 360 Security then connects to the server to complete the updates.

In order to provide you with a better user experience and data support for different functions, we ask you to provide your cell phone IMEI number, phone model information, and the settings for your memory cleanup ignore list. 360 Security matches your provided information against the information below to issue more precise data that helps to enhance your experience with this product.

New Elements of the User Experience Improvement Program

We hope that analysis of this information will enable us to improve the usability, stability, and utilities of 360 Security. Specifically, we hope to:

1) Enhance the usability of 360 Security for simpler and smoother user operation;

2) Ensure the stability, compatibility, aesthetics, and functioning of 360 Security when used with different mobile devices;

3) Improve the effectiveness of memory cleanup, and optimize default settings for the memory cleanup ignore list.

Participation in the User Experience Improvement Program Will Not Infringe On Your Privacy

360 strictly abides by and complies with relevant laws and regulations. The data and information collected by this program is completely anonymous, and we will never match and store collected information against other personal information. Information is only collected as a means to enhance the service quality and efficiency that our product provides to users. All information will be used to improve user experience and you will never receive promotions for other software or services as a result. We will keep such information strictly confidential, and it will never be provided to any third-party companies.

Confirm participation in "360 Securty User Experience Improvement Program"

To indicate your agreement to participate in the 360 Seccurity User Experience Improvement Program, go to "Settings" and change the option "Join the User Experience Improvement Program" to "On".

Opting Out of the Program

It is your decision whether to participate in the 360 Security User Experience Improvement Program. If you would like to opt out of the program, go to "Settings" and change the option "Join the User Experience Improvement Program" to "Off".

On the basis of product development, 360 may make changes to the 360 Seucurity User Experience Improvement Program. Relevant information will be issued via the product and published on a prominent section of our website to ensure that users are promptly notified. If you choose to continue using our services, this implies your consent and agreement with these changes.
