How to Create a Photo Comic

  • Tap the Camera icon to begin making a Photo Comic.
  • Choose a photo: You can either choose a photo from your library or take a photo using your camera. If you snap a photo and don't like it, just tap 'discard' or 'retake' to try again.
  • Add a speech bubble: Tap the Speech Bubble icon to add dialogue. Tap and drag the bubble to change its position. Remove the speech bubble by tapping Remove.
  • Choose your pose: Tap the Smiley Face icon for a library of avatar positions.
  • Share your Photo Comic: Tap the Checkmark in the top right corner for share options.

Can I Create a Photo Comic with a Friend's Avatar?

You can currently only create Photo Comics using your avatar.

Why can't I access my camera or photos to create a photo comic?

You may have denied the Bitstrips app permission to access your camera or photo album. If you want to make a Photo Comic, change these permissions for the Bitstrips app in your device settings.

Making 1-Person and 2-Person Comics

When browsing scenes, you can select who appears in them by tapping the Profile Pics at the top of the page. If only one person has been selected, you'll only see 1-person comics. When you add a friend, you'll see both 1- and 2-person comics.

Customizing Scenes

  • Tapping on any scene will take you to the Customize Scene page.
  • Add a speech bubble: Tap 'Add bubble' to insert a text bubble and write dialogue. Change your facial expression: Tap the smiley-face icon to open the Expressions menu and choose a different facial expression.
  • Customize the caption: Tap on the text below your scene and type new text to create your own caption
  • Switch character positions: When customizing 2-person scenes, you can tap the Swap button in between the 2 profile pics to switch where they appear in the scene.
  • Share your comic: Tap the Checkmark in the top right corner for share options.

Using the Search Bar

From the Make a Comic page, use the search bar at the top of the page to find specific scenes. You can search by keywords, genre, settings, themes, and phrases.

Creating Comics with 3 or More People

Scenes on Bitstrips are currently designed for 1 to 2 people. It's not possible to add more than 2 people to a scene.

Direct Comic Sharing

You can share comics with specific friends from within the Bitstrips app. When you are on the Share page, tap 'Direct' and select which friends you want to share with.

Wheelchairs and Mobility Devices

Including wheelchairs and mobility devices is an ongoing technical challenge for the Bitstrips team. We are sensitive to this issue and hope to find a solution in the future.

Editing/Resetting an Avatar

Edit your avatar from the Profile page, or tap 'Reset my Avatar' from the Settings page to delete your avatar and start from scratch.

If you haven't made changes to your avatar, you will be unable to exit the Avatar Editor. Make a change, and then either save your changes or tap 'X' to exit and cancel changes.

It's not possible to edit a friend's avatar after they have been designed.

Pregnant Avatars, Pet Avatars, Facial Piercings, and Tattoos

Pregnant avatars, pet avatars, facial piercings, and tattoos are popular feature requests, and are among our priorities for future versions of the app.
